							<p>The drop seeps whole <br />
from boulder-lichen <br />
or ledge moss and drops, <br />
<br />
joining, to trickle, <br />
run, fall, dash, <br />
sprawl in held deeps, <br />
<br />
to rush shallows, spill <br />
thin through heights, <br />
but then, edging, <br />
<br />
to eddy aside, nothing <br />
of all but nothing's <br />
curl of motion spent.</p>
