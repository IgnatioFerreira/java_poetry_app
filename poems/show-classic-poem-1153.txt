							<p>After the event the rockslide<br />
realized,<br />
in a still diversity of completion,<br />
grain and fissure,<br />
declivity<br />
&<br />
force of upheaval,<br />
whether rain slippage,<br />
ice crawl, root<br />
explosion or<br />
stream erosive undercut:<br />
<br />
well I said it is a pity:<br />
one swath of sight will never<br />
be the same: nonetheless,<br />
this <br />
shambles has<br />
relived a bind, a taut of twist,<br />
revealing streaks &<br />
scores of knowledge<br />
now obvious and quiet.</p>
