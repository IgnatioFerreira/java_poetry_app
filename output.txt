						<div class="poem-entry" id="contentfont">
							<p>The saints are gathering at the real<br />
places, trying tough skin on sharp<br />
 conscience,<br />
endurance in the hot spots-<br />
searching out to define, come up<br />
against, mouth<br />
the bitterest bit:<br />
you can hear them yelping<br />
down in the dark greeny groves of<br />
 condemnation:<br />
their lips slice back <br />
with jittery suctions, cold<br />
insweeps of conjured grief:<br />
if they, footloose, wham up the<br />
precise damnation,<br />
 consolation<br />
may be more than us trudging<br />
down from paunchy dinners,<br />
swatting hallelujah arms at<br />
dusk bugs and telling them pure<br />
terror has obviously made them<br />
earnest of mind and of motion lithe.</p>
